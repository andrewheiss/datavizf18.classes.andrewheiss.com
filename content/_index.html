 This site contains the syllabus, schedule, and assignments for MPA 635: Data Visualization, held in the first term of Fall 2017 at Brigham Young University.

By the end of this course, you will become (1) literate in data and graphic design principles, (2) an ethical data communicator, and (3) a collaborative sharer by producing beautiful, powerful, and clear visualizations of your own data.
